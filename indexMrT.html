Lifestyle: a sunshine state of mind

Opening Soon

Sat 3 Mar — Sun 21 Oct | SLQ Gallery, level 2, State Library of Queensland
Free exhibition | #SLQlifestyle

Showcasing a range of State Library of Queensland’s rare and restricted collection items, Lifestyle explores how Queenslanders live and play in the sunshine state. Participatory and thought-provoking, it explores the myriad of ideas and identities that are quintessentially Queensland.
